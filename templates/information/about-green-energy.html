About Green Energy

Green energy comes from natural sources like the sun, wind, and water. These sources don’t run out and don’t harm the planet like oil or coal.

Common Types

  • Solar: Uses sunlight to make electricity.
  • Wind: Wind turns big turbines to create power.
  • Hydro: Uses moving water from rivers or dams.

Why It Matters

Green energy helps clean up the air, fight climate change, and save money in the long run. It’s better for the planet and future generations.
