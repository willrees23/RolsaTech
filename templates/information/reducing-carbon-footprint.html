Reducing Your Carbon Footprint

A “carbon footprint” is how much pollution your actions cause. Using green energy, driving less, and wasting less can help lower it.

Simple Tips

  • Turn off lights when you leave a room.
  • Use reusable bags and bottles.
  • Eat less meat and more local food.
  • Take public transport, walk, or bike.
  • Switch to LED bulbs and smart thermostats.

Why It Helps

Less pollution means cleaner air and water, a cooler planet, and better health for everyone.

"Small steps every day lead to big changes."
